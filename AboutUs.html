About Us

Dankatsu started unexpectedly and without preparation. Initially, it was just a small booth for takoyaki. But when we discovered this place, we began planning to add rice dishes to our menu. The inspiration for this came from my husband, Dan, who is a manager at “Shakes.” With every step of our development, our goal has been to provide our customers with a special and unique experience through our unique dishes and warm hospitality.

At Dankatsu, we take pride in our dishes made from the finest ingredients and traditional cooking methods. From delicious takoyaki to rice dishes that will surely satisfy your hunger, each dish is created with love and dedication. We combine fresh ingredients and authentic recipes to ensure that every bite is an unforgettable experience.

When you enter Dankatsu, you’ll immediately feel the warm welcome and cozy ambiance that makes you feel at home. Our friendly staff and manager are always ready to provide the best service to ensure that every guest has an unforgettable experience. Relax, enjoy delicious food, and let us take you on a culinary journey filled with joy and happiness.
